A better way to prepare for college.

GET STARTED

Team up with a student advocate.

The college process should be about helping students find the best-fit opportunities for higher education. Instead, it has become a confusing and stressful process that favors students who can afford expensive private college counselors. We want to fundamentally change the way that students prepare for and apply to college by first arming them with affordable and experienced student advocates who will mentor them through the entire process.

Here is how Strive works.

Step 1

Set-up a free 30-minute phone call with one of our student advocates.

Step 2

Once you sign-up, you'll meet twice a month with your student advocate.

Step 3

The advocate will guide the student through the college process milestones.

Step 4

After being admitted, we will help you plan ahead for your first semester.
